Now you are approaching to the End.
It’s not chaos, nor is it the night,
since the night requires eyes to be seen.
It’s like a blackhole but not entirely.
We shall only call it “End”
because of our barren mind.
Death is just merely a piece of it,
so is reliefs.
The End is an eternal mirror
that God would look at Himself,
and today we might as well.
